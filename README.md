# Analise-De-Churn
----
"Loyal customers are less expensive to serve. They don’t require the same high marketing and acquisition costs as new customers. And they stay longer, buy more, and recommend your business to others."

"When companies focus on making customers happy, retention rates go up, and the cost of doing business goes down. Defection (churn) is a clear sign of customer unhappiness, and it is the single most expensive mistake a company can make."

Reichheld, F. (2020). The Best Way to Cut Costs Is to Make Customers Happy. Net Promoter System - Bain & Company.
